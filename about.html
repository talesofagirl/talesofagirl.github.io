About Me

 

My name is Victoria Nguyen. I'm an artist who uses photography as a tool for introspection and storytelling. My work is centered on ideas of escapism, fantasy, and make-believe associated with anxiety. Through photography, I'm able to create images to investigate my own mental health and peer into my soul.

You can learn more by viewing my CV or following my Instagram at @vittorianguyen.
